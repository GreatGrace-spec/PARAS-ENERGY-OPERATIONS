# Paras Energy Performance Dashboard

A professional interactive Excel dashboard built to monitor and analyze energy generation, revenue, downtime, and operational efficiency across multiple plants.

## 📊 Features

- **Interactive Slicers** for Plant and Month filtering
- **Executive KPI Cards**: Total Revenue, Energy Generated, Operating Surplus & Margin
- **Monthly Trend Analysis** – Track revenue and energy performance over time
- **Plant Comparison** – Identify top and underperforming plants
- **Automated Pivot Tables** with DAX-style calculated fields (Operating Surplus, Margin, etc.)
- Clean, professional design with consistent branding

## 🎯 Key Insights Delivered

- Lagos Plant consistently leads in revenue and efficiency
- Kano Plant shows highest downtime – key area for operational improvement
- Strong overall operating margin of ~41%
- Clear correlation between energy generated and revenue

## 🛠️ Technologies Used

- Microsoft Excel
- Pivot Tables & Slicers
- Calculated Fields

## How to Use

1. Download the repository
2. Open `paras.xlsx`
3. Go to **Dashboard** sheet
4. Use the **Plant** and **Month** slicers to interact with the visuals
